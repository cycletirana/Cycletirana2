Who We Are

Cycle Tirana was born from a love of biking, nature, and the vibrant city of Tirana. Our mission is to offer guided cycling tours that show you the real heartbeat of the city — from street art to historical architecture and hidden gems known only to locals.

We believe in sustainable tourism, active lifestyles, and unforgettable experiences. Whether you're an experienced cyclist or just curious to explore, we’ve got a route and a vibe just for you.

Join us and discover Tirana on two wheels. Let’s ride!
