Cycle at the lake

Tour Description

Join us for a 3-hour sunset cycling experience through Tirana's most scenic and meaningful spots!

  • 📍 Meeting Point – Near Bajram Curri Blvd, close to Blloku.
  • 🚴 Tirana Park / Artificial Lake – Golden hour ride by the lake.

  • 🏛️ The Pyramid – Youth hub and controversial monument.
  • 🗿 Skanderbeg Square – Tirana’s energetic center.
  • 🌿 Rinia Park – Cool down through greenery.
  • 🔁 Return – Back to the start with memories made.

Perfect for travelers, locals, couples, or groups — this tour blends movement, storytelling, and the city's best sunset spot.
